AR Glasses ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience when you visit our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

If you do not agree with the use of cookies as outlined here, you may manage your cookie preferences or stop using the Site.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, like your preferred language or login status, making future visits more seamless and personalized.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They include:

  • Session management
  • Authentication
  • Security

2.2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect data on how users interact with the Site to help us improve functionality. Examples include:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Error tracking

2.3. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering preferences or language settings.

2.4. Targeting & Advertising Cookies

We may use these to deliver ads relevant to your interests and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. These may be set by third-party services such as:

  • Google Ads
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device for analytics, advertising, or functionality purposes. These third parties include but are not limited to:

  • Google Analytics
  • Stripe or PayPal (for secure transactions)
  • Email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp)

Please refer to their respective policies for more information on their use of cookies.

4. Cookie Consent

Upon your first visit to our Site, you will see a cookie banner requesting your consent to use non-essential cookies. You may:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences via the banner or browser settings

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

Instructions for managing cookies are available at:

  • Google Chrome

  • Microsoft Edge
