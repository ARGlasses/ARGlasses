About Rokid

Rokid is a Chinese-based augmented reality company creating innovative and accessible AR eyewear for consumers and professionals alike. Known for its compact form factor and immersive display technology, Rokid’s glasses are ideal for gaming, streaming, and productivity.

Rokid Max

  • Micro-OLED display with up to 215-inch virtual screen
  • Supports 1080p resolution at 120Hz refresh rate
  • Lightweight (75g) and foldable design
  • Plug-and-play with phones, laptops, and gaming consoles via USB-C
  • Myopia adjustment support for users with nearsightedness

Rokid AR Studio

Rokid provides a suite of developer tools including SDKs and an XR platform for spatial apps:

  • Rokid Station: Android-based computing box for AR experiences
  • Gesture and voice control capabilities
  • XR content ecosystem growing in education, retail, and industrial training

Target Markets

  • Gaming and media streaming
  • Work-from-anywhere productivity
  • Travel, personal cinema, and multi-screen mobile use
  • Business presentations and immersive product demos
