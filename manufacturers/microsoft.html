About Microsoft HoloLens

Microsoft is a leader in enterprise and industrial AR through its HoloLens product line, which enables mixed reality experiences for training, collaboration, and hands-free workflows in industries like healthcare, manufacturing, and defense.

HoloLens 2 Key Features

  • Fully self-contained headset with no need for a PC or phone
  • Eye-tracking, hand-gesture control, and voice input
  • Enterprise-level security and Azure integration
  • Wider field of view than original HoloLens
  • High-resolution see-through holographic lenses

Industry Applications

HoloLens is widely used in sectors where remote collaboration and digital overlays improve productivity:

  • Healthcare: Pre-surgical visualization and training
  • Manufacturing: Hands-free instruction, remote assistance
  • Defense: U.S. Army IVAS program partnership
  • Education: Immersive learning environments

Developer Tools

  • HoloLens 2 Hardware Docs
  • Mixed Reality Toolkit (MRTK)
  • Microsoft Mixed Reality Developer Hub

Pricing & Availability

HoloLens 2 is priced at $3,500 and is available primarily to commercial and government buyers. It is distributed through Microsoft and select enterprise resellers.
