About Lenovo’s AR Strategy

Lenovo is a growing force in the AR space, focusing on business and industrial applications through its ThinkReality platform and AR devices such as the ThinkReality A3 Smart Glasses.

ThinkReality A3 Smart Glasses

  • Lightweight smart glasses tethered to a Lenovo PC or select Motorola smartphones
  • 3D visualization of data, apps, and virtual monitors
  • Private display for professionals on the go or in factory settings
  • 8MP RGB camera and dual fish-eye cameras for room-scale tracking

ThinkReality Ecosystem

Lenovo’s ThinkReality platform provides a scalable software environment for deploying AR solutions:

  • Supports multiple XR devices (not limited to Lenovo hardware)
  • Remote assistance, workflow guidance, and 3D training
  • Cloud-managed and enterprise secure

Target Industries

  • Remote field support and maintenance
  • Manufacturing and logistics
  • Healthcare and technical training
  • Finance and enterprise productivity

The ThinkReality A3 is available to business clients only and typically priced around $1,499–$2,499 depending on configuration and deployment tools.
