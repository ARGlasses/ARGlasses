About Meta’s AR Journey

Meta (formerly Facebook) is investing heavily in the future of augmented and mixed reality through products like the Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses and its long-term development platform, Project Nazare.

Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses (2023)

  • Developed in partnership with Ray-Ban and EssilorLuxottica
  • Features a 12MP camera, open-ear audio, and voice assistant integration
  • Capture and livestream from a first-person perspective
  • Touchpad on the arm and "Hey Meta" voice control

Meta’s Vision for AR

Meta’s long-term roadmap includes full-featured AR glasses that overlay digital content directly onto your environment — codenamed Project Nazare. While still under development, this technology is expected to power immersive productivity, social interaction, and spatial apps powered by Meta’s Reality Labs.

Meta Platforms & Tools

  • Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses
  • Reality Labs Research Blog
  • Spark AR Studio (Meta’s AR platform)

Developer Support

Meta offers Spark AR as a platform to create AR experiences across Facebook and Instagram, and is actively building out a broader AR OS and app ecosystem.

Pricing & Availability

Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses start at $299 and are available in various styles and colors. More advanced AR prototypes are not yet consumer-ready.
