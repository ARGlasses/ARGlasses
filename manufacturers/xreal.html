About XREAL (Formerly Nreal)

XREAL is a pioneering AR company delivering stylish and portable mixed reality glasses designed for media, gaming, and productivity. With a strong developer focus and rapidly expanding device support, XREAL is reshaping how consumers access spatial content.

Top Models: XREAL Air 2 & Air 2 Pro

  • Micro-OLED display, up to 330-inch virtual screen at 1080p
  • AR Spatial Display with motion tracking and head-locked visuals
  • Compatible with phones, laptops, gaming consoles, and drones
  • Lightweight (~72g), travel-friendly design with comfort in mind
  • Pro version supports electrochromic dimming (AR to VR blend)

XREAL Beam & Spatial OS

XREAL Beam is an accessory and computing unit that powers spatial tracking, gesture control, and screen casting. It transforms XREAL Air into a fully immersive AR experience:

  • 3DoF motion tracking
  • Spatial anchors and environmental awareness
  • USB-C and wireless connectivity

Real-World Use Cases

Pricing & Availability

XREAL Air 2 retails for $399, and the Beam sells for an additional $119. Available via XREAL.com, Amazon, and Best Buy in select regions.
